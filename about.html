What is this app

COVID-19 continues to have catastrophic effects on the lives of human beings
throughout the world.
To combat this disease it is necessary to screen the affected patients in a fast and inexpensive way.
Chest X-Ray being the most easily available and least expensive option.
a stand-in approach to detect the disease can be radiography scanning, where chest radiography images can be analyzed to detect the symptoms of the novel coronavirus .
The application provides an easy way to predict if the person exhibits covid-19 symptoms or not
